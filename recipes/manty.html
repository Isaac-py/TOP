Кыргыз Манты (Kyrgyz Manty)

Description

Manti are traditional Kyrgyz steamed dumplings filled with juicy meat and onions, wrapped in thin dough, and cooked in a special steamer called a mantovarka.

Ingredients

  • 500g flour
  • 200ml water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 500g minced lamb or beef
  • 300g onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 50g butter (optional, for extra juiciness)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water, and salt to make a smooth dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, mix minced meat, onions, black pepper, cumin, and butter.
  3. Roll out the dough and cut it into squares (about 10x10 cm).
  4. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each square.
  5. Fold the dough, pinching the edges to form dumplings.
  6. Steam the manti in a mantovarka for about 40-45 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with sour cream or a spicy tomato sauce.
