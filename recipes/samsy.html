Kyrgyz Samsy (Кыргыз Самсасы)

Description

Samsy are Kyrgyz baked pastries filled with spiced meat and onions, wrapped in flaky dough, and traditionally baked in a tandoor oven.

Ingredients

  • 500g flour
  • 250ml water
  • 100g butter or margarine
  • 500g minced lamb or beef
  • 300g onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg yolk (for brushing)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water, and melted butter to form a dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, combine minced meat, onions, black pepper, cumin, and salt.
  3. Roll out the dough and cut it into circles.
  4. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle.
  5. Fold the dough into a triangle or half-moon shape, sealing the edges.
  6. Brush the tops with egg yolk for a golden finish.
  7. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C (392°F) for 30-35 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with tea or coke. Some people might serve with Shorpo, traditional Kyrgyz soup.
